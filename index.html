Hi, I'm Kuat Telegenov

Robotics Engineer

I'm a passionate Robotics Engineer currently contributing my expertise to KAUST's Robotics Intelligent Systems and Control Lab. My journey in this field began at Nazarbayev University, where I enriched young minds as an Instructor in advanced robotics courses.

I specialize in robotics research and development. My projects include underwater robots for monitoring coral reefs, designing autonomous drones, and working on remotely operated vehicles. My skills are in control theory, aerodynamics, 3D modeling, and additive manufacturing. I'm experienced in using different programming languages, ROS2, and various engineering tools.

I believe in always learning more. I've earned certifications in areas like aerial robotics, self-driving cars, deep learning and many more from online platforms.

I've taken on leadership roles, like leading teams in international robotics competitions and managing a research lab. I've also published several research papers in different field of robotics.

For more details about my background and experience, please have a look at my resume.
